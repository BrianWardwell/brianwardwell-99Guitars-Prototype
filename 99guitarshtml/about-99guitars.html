ABOUT 99 GUITARS


99 Guitars was founded in 1968 and began with the staples such as Fender and Gibson. Since then, guitars and guitar companies have drastically expaneded. We carry over 20 major brand of guitars, amps, effects and accessories. Not only carrying Fender and Gibson, but also Jackson, Schecter, Ibanez, Taylor, Ovation, Yamaha, Hamer, Peavey, and many more. We pride ourselves in trying to offer the same variety in amplifiers as well: Peavey, Marshall, Randall, Fender, Mesa-Boogie, and more. Stop in today to browse our wide selection and we encourage asking any questions that you may have to our friendly and knowledgable staff.
